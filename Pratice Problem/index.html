Welcome to Our Departments

SSUET offers a variety of programs across engineering, technology, and sciences. Each department is equipped with modern labs, experienced faculty, and a focus on research and innovation.

Department of Computer Science

The Department of Computer Science focuses on computing theory, programming, artificial intelligence, and data science. It prepares students for modern technological challenges through innovative learning and research.

Department of Information Technology

The Department of IT emphasizes information systems, networking, cybersecurity, and database management. Students gain hands-on experience in managing and designing technology-driven solutions.

Department of Software Engineering

This department focuses on software design, development, and project management. It trains students in modern software tools, methodologies, and best practices to meet global standards.

Department of Electrical Engineering

The Electrical Engineering department provides strong foundations in power systems, electronics, and telecommunication. Students gain both theoretical and practical experience through advanced lab facilities.

Department of Civil Engineering

The Civil Engineering department offers expertise in construction, structural design, and environmental engineering. Students work on practical projects and gain real-world exposure through field training.
