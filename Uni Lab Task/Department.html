Departments

Department of Computer Science

The Department of Computer Science at SSUET focuses on core computing concepts, algorithms,
and software development. Students learn programming, artificial intelligence, data science, and
modern computing technologies. The department aims to prepare graduates for both research and
industry innovation.

Department of Information Technology

The Department of Information Technology emphasizes the application of computing in business,
networking, and information systems. Courses cover topics like database management,
network security, cloud computing,and IT project management, ensuring students can design
and manage technology-driven solutions in real-world environments.

Department of Software Engineering

The Department of Software Engineering prepares students to design, build,
and maintain reliable software systems. The curriculum blends programming, software architecture,
project management, and quality assurance to develop engineers capable of leading
software development teams and creating robust applications.
